About

The Monkees are an American rock band originally active between 1966 and 1971. Their line-up consists of musicians Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, Peter Tork and Davy Jones.
Their style is a combination of rock and psychodelic rock with folk elements. After reuniting in the early 2000s, they currently play at events and occasionally tour the world.
